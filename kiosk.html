How It Works

Four steps. Zero hesitation.

01

Deploy

Unpack from kit. VACTOR is configured and ready for use within seconds — no assembly, no setup delay when time is the variable.

02

Seat the Pump Head

Attach the pump head with a single-click lock. Tactile confirmation. Designed to be done under stress, in the dark, with gloves on.

03

Activate

Power on. Begin medical-grade aspiration immediately. Consistent, reliable suction across all operating conditions without a power grid.

04

Clear

Maintain a clear airway or surgical field. Replace the pump head in the field and continue operations. The mission does not pause.
